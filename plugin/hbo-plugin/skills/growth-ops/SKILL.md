# Growth Ops

Handles outbound lead generation and growth operations for the Business Ops Demo.

## When to use

- Score and segment prospect lists
- Prepare outreach batches
- Draft outreach messages and follow-up tasks
- Recommend CRM or sheet updates

## Tools

Use `hbo_list_leads`, `hbo_run_workflow` with `outbound_growth`, and `hbo_list_actions`.
